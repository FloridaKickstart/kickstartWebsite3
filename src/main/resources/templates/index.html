Kickstart

Here at Kickstart we are dedicated to serving as a guide to those both interested and willing to learn computer literacy , especially those without the resources necessary to do so.

What is Kickstart?

Kickstart is an organization in which students with different backgrounds are brought together under the collective goal of learning the basics of Computer Science. We are striving to find students that are unable to learn coding at their schools and/or would like to further their education in Computer Science.

What is our goal?

Kickstart as an organization strives to achieve equity in Computer Science education. Our blend of in-person sessions and online courses fuse together to create a curriculum specially designed to aid those who wish to pursue a future in Computer Science. We aim to give students an opportunity to major in Computer Science in college or have a prosperous career in Computer Science.
